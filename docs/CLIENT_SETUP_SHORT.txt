AirTrainer — Quick Start for Testing
=====================================

1. WEB APP (any device, any browser)
   URL: https://airtrainr.com
   Log in with the test accounts we shared (athlete / trainer / admin).
   Admin email: kayum@gmail.com (password sent separately).
   Or click Sign Up to create fresh accounts.

2. ANDROID APP (phone install)
   Download link:
   https://expo.dev/accounts/kayumkhansayal/projects/AirTrainrApp/builds/6ab2e596-c378-40a3-8c14-047264706321

   Steps:
   a) Open the link on your Android phone in Chrome.
   b) Tap "Install" / "Download" to get the APK file.
   c) Open the downloaded APK. If Android warns about "unknown source",
      tap Settings > toggle "Allow from this source" ON > go back.
   d) Tap Install, then Open.
   e) Log in with test credentials OR Sign Up as a new user.
   f) Allow location, camera and notification permissions when asked.

3. iOS (iPhone)
   Not ready yet. Please use the web app on iPhone for now.

4. WHAT TO TEST
   - Sign up (athlete and trainer)
   - Create / edit profile, upload photo
   - Search trainers, filter by sport and location
   - Send a training offer, edit it, accept / decline as trainer
   - Book and pay (use test card we shared)
   - Chat / send messages and images
   - Leave a review after a session
   - Check notifications arrive

5. REPORTING BUGS
   Email: support@digitalheroes.co.in
   Please include: what you were doing, what went wrong,
   a screenshot, your phone model or browser, and the
   account email you used.

Full guide with troubleshooting: docs/CLIENT_SETUP_INSTRUCTIONS.md
