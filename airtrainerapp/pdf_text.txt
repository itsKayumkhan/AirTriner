4. Athlete Onboarding Flow  
Athletes sign up for free and are matched with verified local coaches based on their sport, 
availability, and goals.  
Steps:  
1. Create Athlete Profile — enter age, sport, skill level, location, travel radius, and preferred 
training times (morning / afternoon /  evening).  
2. Set Training Preferences — choose session type: 1 -on-1 or group, skill -specific 
development, strength & conditioning, pre -season or in -season training.  
3. Get Matched With Verified Trainers — AirTrainr recommends trainers by sport, skill level, 
availability, and distance.  
4. Receive Training Offers — trainers send personalized offers including session types, pricing 
packages, and available time slots.  
5. Book a Session — select date, time, training location, and session length. Payment handled 
securely in -app. 
6. Train & Track Progress — rate and review trainers after sessions; view feedback, progress 
notes, and rebook.  
7. Stay Organised — instant notifications, calendar sync, training reminders, and preferred 
time-of-day matching.  
 
5. Trainer Onboarding Flow  
Trainers create a verified professional profile, set their schedule and pricing, and begin receiving 
matched athlete leads and booking requests.

6.4  Founding 50 Pro gramme  
• Special badge displayed on trainer profiles for the first 50 trainers who join  
• Founding 50 trainers receive 6 months free subscription (instead of standard 7 -day trial)  
• Badge and free subscription are manually assigned by the admin — not automatic  
• Admin controls who receives Founding 50 status via the admin panel  
• Once 50 trainers have been granted Founding 50 status, the offer is closed

6.6  Payment Flow  
• Athlete selects a trainer and books a session.  
• Athlete is charged the trainer's session fee plus a 3% AirTrainr platform fee via Stripe.  
• Payment is held in the platform's Stripe account.  
• After the session is marked complete, the trainer's net amount (session fee minus any 
applicable Stripe fees) is transferred to the trainer's Stripe Connect account on a daily basis.  
• Trainers have full tran saction history and downloadable reports in their dashboard.  
6.7  Admin Panel  
• View and manage all athletes on the platform  
• View and manage all trainers (including verification status)

• Login credentials provided to client
